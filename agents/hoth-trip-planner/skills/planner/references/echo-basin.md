# Echo Basin region

The Echo Basin is Hoth's sheltered southern valley, famous for geothermal
springs under the ice shelf. Best for spa and wellness trips.

## Sites

### Echo Base Thermal Springs
- Operator: Rebel Alliance Leisure
- Type: geothermal spa
- Offers: hot-spring bathing in carved ice grottos, tauntaun-wool towel
  service, post-blizzard recovery soaks.

### Wampa Ridge Spa
- Operator: Imperial Wellness
- Type: luxury spa
- Offers: cryo-sauna, ice-crystal facials, guided cave meditation (wampa-free
  since 3 ABY).

### Shield Generator Vista Baths
- Operator: Rebel Alliance Leisure
- Type: panorama pools
- Offers: heated outdoor pools with a view over the old shield generator
  plain; evening aurora watching.
