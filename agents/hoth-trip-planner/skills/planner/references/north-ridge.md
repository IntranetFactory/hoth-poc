# North Ridge region

The North Ridge is Hoth's high alpine spine — steep couloirs, reliable
powder, and the planet's only groomed pistes. Best for ski trips.

## Sites

### North Ridge Piste Lodge
- Operator: Rebel Alliance Leisure
- Type: ski resort
- Offers: 14 groomed pistes, snowspeeder-served backcountry drops, ski-in
  cantina.

### Imperial Glacier Ski Arena
- Operator: Imperial Wellness
- Type: ski resort
- Offers: floodlit night skiing on the glacier, AT-AT sightseeing lift,
  race-training slalom lanes.

### Tauntaun Flats Nordic Centre
- Operator: Rebel Alliance Leisure
- Type: cross-country ski centre
- Offers: 40 km of classic and skate tracks across the flats, tauntaun
  sledding for kids.
